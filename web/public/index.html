Phishing E-Mail Spam Classifier

Use Watson Natural Language Classifier to predict e-mail spam. Click the drop down to view some examples or enter your own email text and see how its classified.

The Training Data: Data files are a modification of the Enron E-mail Dataset as found on EDRM.net. That data is available under a Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported License.
